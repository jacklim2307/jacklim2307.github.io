FAQ

Q. How do purchase my order on the site?
To order your purchase :-

  1. Click “Add to cart” button on the products you wish to purchase.
  2. Click “Cart” to proceed your payment for the products.
  3. You'll reach a log in section where you can log in your account using your Username and Password. If you don't have an account created yet, you may register an account before you proceed your payment.
  4. You'll need to fill in your detail on the “Payment” page and click “Pay” button.
  5. After the steps before this have been done, You need to reconfirm your billing details and agree it.
  6. Click “ok” to complete the order.
